Privacy Policy for No Shorts Video

Last Updated: February 8, 2025

1. Data Collection

No Shorts Video does not collect, store, or share any personal or usage data. This includes:

  • No browsing history
  • No IP addresses or location data
  • No credentials or personal information
  • No content from websites (e.g., YouTube/Instagram videos, comments, etc.)

2. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API to save user preferences locally on your device:

  • Enabled/Disabled Status (toggle switch)
  • Platform Selection (YouTube Shorts/Instagram Reels blocking)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or third parties.

3. Permissions

  • youtube.com and instagram.com Access: Required to modify page content (hide Shorts/Reels elements)
  • declarativeNetRequest: Used solely to redirect YouTube Shorts URLs to regular video pages

4. Third-Party Services

Donate (Ko-fi) and Telegram Links: These buttons open external websites. We do not control their content or privacy practices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer data to anyone, as no data is collected.

6. Compliance

This extension is not affiliated with YouTube, Instagram, or their parent companies.

We comply with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the extension implies acceptance of the revised policy.
